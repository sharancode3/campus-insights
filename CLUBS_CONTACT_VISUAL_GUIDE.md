├── Research/Innovation: 2 columns
├── Contact Form: 2 columns  
├── Contact Details: 4 columns
└── Department Contacts: 4 columns

├── Clubs Grid: 2 columns
├── Research/Innovation: 2 columns
├── Contact Form: 2 columns
├── Contact Details: 2 columns
└── Department Contacts: 2 columns

Mobile (<920px)
├── Clubs Grid: 1 column
├── All Grids: 1 column
├── Contact Form: 1 column (stacked)
├── Contact Details: 1 column
└── Map & Form: Stacked vertically
