About Department

Established in the year 1946, the Department of Civil Engineering at B.M.S College of Engineering has progressively achieved academic excellence, quality research and consultancy for over period of seven decades. The focus of the Department is to impart quality technical education in Civil Engineering holistically in order to meet professional challenges in the society with required competency.

The Department receives grant from government of Karnataka and offers B.E, M.Tech and Ph.D programmes which are globally recognized. The undergraduate B.E program was accredited for 3 years under Tier-I (Washington Accord) in 2022, recognizing our academic achievements and is continuously accredited till date. PG programs are also accredited by NBA.

The sanctioned intake for UG currently is 120 and 18 for PG in each program. From the year 2008, UG program has been receiving grant from AICTE, New Delhi as per NI status and infrastructure has been upgraded with latest equipment's and laboratory setup.

Syllabus

The curriculum is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge in both traditional and emerging areas of Civil Engineering. It includes:

  • Core subjects in Structural, Geotechnical, Water Resources Engineering
  • Transportation engineering and environmental engineering
  • Electives in advanced topics and research areas
  • Practical laboratory work and project-based learning

Faculty

Our faculty comprises experienced academicians with Ph.D. degrees, industry expertise, and active research publications. They are committed to mentoring students and advancing the field through research.
