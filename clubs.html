Club Activities

Programs Conducted by Student Communities

Club calendars combine recurring practice sessions with flagship events and domain-specific initiatives.

Workshops and Skill Studios

Hands-on workshops covering coding, design systems, robotics assembly, storytelling, and entrepreneurship modules.

  • Weekly technical labs for coding, AI model pipelines, and embedded prototyping.
  • Design and communication workshops focusing on UX, visual systems, and presentations.
  • Performance arts and cultural practice sessions led by senior student coordinators.

Hackathons and Competitions

Competitive formats that test execution under constraints while fostering collaboration and leadership.

  • Campus and inter-college hackathons with domain mentors and jury evaluation frameworks.
  • Case challenge series for entrepreneurship, analytics, and consulting pathways.
  • Sports leagues and cultural circuits that build consistency, discipline, and teamwork.

Guest Sessions and Industry Talks

Sessions with alumni, founders, artists, and specialists who provide contextual insights and career direction.

  • Alumni knowledge sessions on roles, tools, and project pathways in industry.
  • Founder and product talks through the innovation and incubation ecosystem.
  • Cultural masterclasses from performing artists and professional curators.

Community and Outreach Programs

Students engage with schools, civic organizations, and community initiatives through service projects.

  • Literacy and digital awareness initiatives in nearby communities.
  • Sustainability drives including waste management and campus greening programs.
  • Health and wellness campaigns in partnership with student support teams.
