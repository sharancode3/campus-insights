How It Works

Simple, Transparent Application Timeline

Step 1: Eligibility Check

Review program requirements and ensure you meet academic qualifications and prerequisites for your chosen program.

Step 2: Online Application

Complete our online application portal with personal information, academic records, and essays about your goals.

Step 3: Document Submission

Submit required documents including transcripts, test scores, and recommendation letters through secure portal.

Step 4: Interview

Shortlisted candidates attend interviews (online or in-person) to discuss academic and career aspirations.

Step 5: Admission Decision

Receive admission decision within 2-3 weeks of final interview. Acceptance includes enrollment details and deadlines.

Step 6: Enrollment

Complete enrollment process by submitting confirmation, fees, and attending orientation before semester begins.

Program Requirements

What We're Looking For

Undergraduate Eligibility

  • 12th grade completion or equivalent
  • Minimum 75% aggregate or equivalent CGPA
  • Valid entrance exam score (JEE Main/State CET)
  • English language proficiency
  • No pending legal proceedings
  • Age minimum 17 years at admission

Postgraduate Eligibility

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Minimum 60% or 6.5 CGPA in UG
  • Valid GATE/GMAT score (program dependent)
  • Statement of purpose and motivation
  • Academic recommendations from professors
  • Relevant work experience preferred

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application fee?

Application fee is Rs. 1,500 for domestic students and $50 for international students. Fee is non-refundable and must be submitted with online application.

When are admissions open?

UG admissions: June-July and December-January. PG admissions: April-May and September-October. PhD admissions open year-round with rolling basis decisions.

Can I apply for multiple programs?

Yes, you can apply for up to 3 different programs in a single application. However, you must accept only one admission offer.

How long is the admission process?

Typically 3-4 weeks from application submission to decision notification. Priority processing available for early applicants.

Do you accept international qualifications?

Yes. We accept IB, IGCSE, A-Levels, and qualifications from recognized international boards. Conversion certificates may be required.

Is accommodation provided?

Yes. On-campus hostel accommodations available on first-come-first-serve basis. Off-campus residence support also provided.
