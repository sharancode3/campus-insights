About BMSCE

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1946

B.M.S. College of Engineering (BMSCE) was founded in 1946 by the visionary Late Sri. B. M. Sreenivasaiah, making it the first private sector initiative in engineering education in India. Over 75+ years, BMSCE has been dedicated to providing world-class engineering education and groundbreaking research.

Today, BMSCE offers 13 Undergraduate and 16 Postgraduate programs across conventional and emerging areas of engineering. The college is accredited by prestigious bodies including NAAC (A++), NBA, VTU, and AICTE, and consistently ranks among the top engineering institutions in India.

BMSCE's commitment extends beyond academics to encompass research, innovation, and social responsibility. Our 70+ years of excellence have produced thousands of alumni who now lead global organizations.

Every student journey at BMSCE is shaped by world-class mentorship, collaborative learning, and cutting-edge infrastructure designed to foster innovation and leadership.
