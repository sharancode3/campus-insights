Placement Ecosystem

Integrated Support System for Career Progression

Placement success is delivered through coordinated units across training, exposure, mentorship, and employer engagement. Each unit manages a defined stage of candidate readiness and recruitment outcomes.

Placement Cell

Coordinates recruiter outreach, drive scheduling, eligibility management, and interview logistics through structured communication with students and employers.

Career Development

Delivers resume audits, role mapping, communication labs, and profile-building modules aligned with current hiring expectations across domains.

Internship Program

Supports pre-placement internships through semester-wise matching, project mentoring, and conversion pipelines for full-time opportunities.

Industry Mentorship

Domain experts from partner organizations guide interview preparation, role expectation understanding, and case problem-solving approaches.

Skill Development

Multi-track modules in DSA, coding platforms, aptitude, technical interviews, and workplace communication ensure technical and professional preparedness.

Alumni Network

Alumni mentors contribute referrals, role-specific sessions, and mock interview panels that improve confidence and conversion across placement rounds.

Placement Process

Structured Placement Journey from Assessment to Offer

Every student follows a process-led progression that begins with capability mapping and ends with job offers aligned to profile, specialization, and career goals.

Step 01

Skill Assessment

Baseline tests evaluate aptitude, programming, core fundamentals, communication, and readiness gaps.

Step 02

Training Programs

Role-specific training tracks are mapped to IT, core engineering, analytics, and consulting pathways.

Step 03

Resume Workshops

Students refine resumes and project narratives with faculty and industry reviewers.

Step 04

Mock Interviews

Panel simulations covering technical, behavioral, and case rounds improve interview readiness.

Step 05

Pre-placement Talks

Recruiters share role scope, hiring expectations, compensation structures, and growth pathways.

Step 06

Placement Drives

Campus recruitment drives are executed with screening, interviews, and final shortlist publication.

Step 07

Final Offers

Offer management, acceptance support, and onboarding guidance are completed by placement mentors.
